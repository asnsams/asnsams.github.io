​SignBD-Word: Bangla Word Level Sign Language Open Access Video Database and Pose Translation

​The SignBD-Word dataset includes 6000 video clips of 200 distinct classes. There are 30 video clips for each of the sign class representing a word. Out of this 200 signs, 110 are one-handed signs, and the remaining signs are two-handed. The words are selected among 15 different general vocabulary categories in order to ensure that the most colloquial expressions in BdSL are incorporated into the dataset.

​This repository contains 50,000 unique synthetic fingerprints generated using HQ-finGAN approach. The dataset is divided into 5 folders, each folder contains 10,000 distinct fingerprints of size 256*256.

​Bangla LPDB - A Dataset

​Bangla LPDB-A is the first complete publicly available dataset of Bangladeshi vehicle images with visible Bangla license plates. The vehicle image dataset consists of almost all types of vehicles present in Bangladesh, excluding army, police, and government vehicles. The images are in JPG, and PNG format. This dataset is of two parts-1. Bangladeshi vehicle images with visible Bangla license plates which includes 1928 images.2. Cropped Bangla license plate which includes 2662 images (720 synthetic images, 1942 images manually cropped from part-1). Each image also contains its corresponding annotated text file in YOLO format. For Part-1 and Part-2, number of classes are 1 and 102, respectively.
