I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois Chicago, working under the supervision of Dr. Besma Smida. Before joining UIC in the Fall of 2023, I served as a Lecturer at Brac University and the Bangladesh University of Textiles from July 2022 to June 2023. I completed my undergraduate studies at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology, where I was advised by Dr. S. M. Mahbubur Rahman.

I am conducting research on Integrated Sensing and Communication with a focus on 6G technology.
