Interested in 
Wireless Communication | Integrated Sensing & Communication 

I am currently doing my research ​under Dr. Besma Smida in Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC) with a focus on 6G wireless technology, bringing together knowledge from Wireless Communication, Estimation Theory, Information Theory, Signal Processing and Deep Learning. My research is inherently multidisciplinary, combining theory with practical implementation to solve emerging challenges in next-generation networks. I am currently working in three different projects- 

(1) Finding fundamental trade-off limits of ISAC in practical real-world scenarios, 
(2) Secondary Communication signal recovery at high interference 
(3) Mixed-domain wireless design for FR3 domain.
