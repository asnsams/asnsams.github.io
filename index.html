I am currently a Ph.D. student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois Chicago, working under the supervision of Dr. Besma Smida as part of the Wireless lab. Before joining UIC in the Fall of 2023, I served as a Lecturer at Brac University and the Bangladesh University of Textiles from July 2022 to June 2023. I completed my undergraduate studies at the Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology.

Resume
 

  Oct 28, 2024: ​Presented our research at the Asilomar Conference on Signals, Systems, and Computers discussing our ongoing project on Integrated Sensing and Communication.
   May 13, 2024: Passed two areas of Ph.D. Qualifying Exam on Communication and Signal Processing. 

I am conducting research on Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC) with a focus on 6G technology. My interests lie boradly in the intersection of Wireless Communication, Estimation Theory, Information Theory, and Signal Processing. Right now, I am exploring the fundamental performance limitations of ISAC systems. My previous experience includes working on various projects related to deep learning and Generative Adversarial Networks.
